My story


I've been tinkering with computer hardware/software since I was 10. My journey into the fascination of technology started when I found a game development website called, "Scratch". I used to spend many hours during breaks in school playing around with code and designing levels. It all came to a head when my class began working on a big English class project. I decided I would develop two short games to coincide with my essay about a book we were reading. About two weeks later, I presented my work to the class and my teacher loved it. From then on, I would build and use my software programming knowledge to create games, small websites and even shot some videos. I had so much fun doing this all throughout middle school and yearned to be some kind of programmer when I was an adult.
...
Fast forward a couple years, I had decided to finish my high school education online after my Freshman year in a public high school. During this time, I found a community-run online course called, the "Odin Project". It's an online web development course and seemed like my kind of thing. I signed up and spent the next 6 - 8 months learning several programming languages and web design techniques through the course. The programs I've learned include: JavaScript, jQuery, SQL, CSS, C#, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, git and HTML. I completed the course and am now currently seeking an employment opportunity as a web developer. It's been a long, and often arduous, journey into web development, but I have come out as a stronger developer and person. I feel prepared and qualified to take on the role as a full-time web developer, and I'm incredibly excited to see what the future holds!
